Mother Teresa

Here's some interesting facts about Mother Teresa's life:

  • By blood she was Albanian. By citizenship, an Indian. By faith, she was a Catholic nun. As to her calling, she belonged to the world. As to her heart, she belonged entirely to the Heart of Jesus.
  • She described herself as "a little pencil in the hand of a writing God who is sending a love letter to the world."
  • Mother Teresa was awarded with the Nobel Peace Prize in 1979.
